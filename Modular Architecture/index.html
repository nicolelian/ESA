Modular Architecture

Abstract modules Functions Message flow between modules
Playermovement Module It tracks the use of die and players’ movement.
  1. Players roll a die to move on the board.
  2. The result from the board module will determine player’s next movement.
  3. Reward submodule tells the player to move one more time.
  4. Penalty submodule tells player to stop moving for one round.
  5. The board module is based on knowledge module, as the “fun-shaped” board is made up of question cards.
  6. The more tokens that are collected, the more knowledge our players are!
  7. The result of player’s answer determines whether he receives reward or penalty.
  8. These three submodules are implements of knowledge module for more varieties, which we may apply in the future. (shown in product family as well)
Board Module The board is made up of question cards. When players land on cards of different colors, they need to answer the question on the card.
Reward Submodule If player answers the question correctly, he can collect token from the square if there is one.
Penalty Submodule If player answers the question wrongly, he will lose one chance of rolling die.
Knowledge Module Knowledge module controls all the cards. We need a system to check the players’ answers whether they are correct.
Country Submodule The question cards are firstly about Singapore property market. We can add other countries’ in the future as well.
Difficulty Submodule The question cards can be divided into different difficulty levels.
Subject Submodule In the future we may expand the topic of question cards from Singapore property market to investment market. Other possible topics include How to start a family
