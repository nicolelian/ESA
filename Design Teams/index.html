Design Teams

Subteams Responsibilities Possible Conflicts Possible Solutions
Product Strategists Product strategists are responsible for game rules, game strategy, solutions to possible conflicts and serve additionally as the link between Knowledge Masters and Artifact Designers. The parameters that product strategists set might be incompatible with artifact designers’ design. Product Strategists need to make compatible designs for Artifact designers to work with.
Knowledge Masters Knowledge masters will prepare all the questions that are going to be written on question cards. They will make sure that the questions are of an appropriate difficulty level, and can be varied in future product family design. There might be a delay in the manufacture of question cards from Artifact designers. Knowledge Masters should coordinate with Artifact Designers regarding the timeline.
Artifact Designers Design the appearance of game board, tokens and question cards in addition to manufacturing and testing all components. The design from Artifact Designers cannot meet the requirements from Product Strategists. Artifact Designers should fulfill Product strategists’ requirements before manufacture.
