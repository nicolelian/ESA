Non-functional Requirements

  1. In 9 out of 10 cases, the gameplay shall not exceed 30 minutes in normal mode with 2 adult players.
  2. The prototype material costs shall not exceed SGD 20.
  3. The game instructions and content should be sufficiently clear to allow for smooth play.
  4. The game equipment (game board, tokens, question cards) should be conveniently portable and recyclable.
  5. The knowledge module shall be capable of offering different difficulty level options for players.
